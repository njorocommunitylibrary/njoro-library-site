Welcome to Njoro Community Library

Empowering our community through knowledge and access to information.

About Us

Njoro Community Library is a community-driven initiative under the Rift Valley Reading Association (RVRA), aiming to promote literacy, digital skills, and lifelong learning in Njoro and surrounding areas.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of knowledge and learning that empowers every community member to thrive in a knowledge-driven society.

Our Mission

To promote inclusive reading culture, information access, and digital literacy through quality library services, outreach programs, and community engagement.

Our Core Values

  • 📖 Literacy and Learning for All
  • 🌍 Equity and Inclusion
  • 🤝 Community Empowerment
  • 📚 Knowledge Sharing
  • 💡 Innovation and Curiosity

Our Services

  • 📚 Book Lending
  • 💻 Free Internet Access
  • 📖 Quiet Reading Spaces
  • 🧒 Children's Storytime
