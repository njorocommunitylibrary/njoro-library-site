Library Membership

Becoming a member of the Njoro Community Library gives you access to a wide range of books, computer services, digital training, and exclusive events. Membership is open to all residents of Njoro and surrounding areas.

Membership Categories

  • Junior Member: Ages 5–17 – Access to the children's collection and events
  • Adult Member: Ages 18–59 – Full access to services, book lending, and programs
  • Senior Member: Age 60+ – Priority access and community wellness programs

Benefits of Membership

  • 📚 Borrow books and learning materials
  • 💻 Free access to computers and internet
  • 👩‍🏫 Participate in workshops and ICT training
  • 🎯 Attend community events and clubs
  • 📨 Get updates and newsletters
