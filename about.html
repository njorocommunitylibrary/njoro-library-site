About Njoro Community Library

Njoro Community Library is a project under the Rift Valley Reading Association (RVRA), established to serve the people of Njoro and neighboring communities with access to books, technology, and knowledge resources. Located in Kwa Chief, Njoro Sub-county, our goal is to cultivate a reading culture, digital empowerment, and inclusive learning spaces for all ages.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of knowledge and learning that empowers every community member to thrive in a knowledge-driven society.

Our Mission

To promote inclusive reading culture, information access, and digital literacy through quality library services, outreach programs, and community engagement.
